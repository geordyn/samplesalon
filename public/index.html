ENCINITAS, CALIFORNIA

Who We Are ⇁

We are a full-service salon centrally located in Wallingford, CT. We have been in business for over 25 years. Specializing in the art of color, including corrective color and dimensional foil highlighting, we strive to enhance our clients' inner and outer beauty.

We strive to have a fun, uplifting, friendly atmosphere with some of the most professional hair designers around. When in our salon, you'll be treated with great service. We give thorough and expert consultations before the start of your service. You'll know exactly what to expect and what you're getting before the start of your service.

What We Do ⇁

Services
View our full line of hair, skin care and nail services. Cut & Color, Brazilian Blowouts, Hair Extensions, Facials, Waxing, Minx nails and more.

Spa Etiquette
For leisurely enjoyment of your full treatment please arrive 15-minutes prior to your appointment. Please note that your scheduled time is your table time. Due to adjacent appointments late arrivals will only be given the remainder of the time originally scheduled for the service.

*Services and prices are subject to change without notice.
